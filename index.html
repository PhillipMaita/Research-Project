Splits

Before presenting the different splits popularized in the world of bodybuilding, what is a split. Very simply a split is another word for schedule. Its usually split up by like 2 or 3 days and repeats with a rest day inbetween (Ex. Push, pull, legs repeats once with one rest day).

Examples

    Push, Pull, Legs:

    Monday

    • Task 1
    • Task 2
    • Task 3

    Tuesday

    • Task A
    • Task B
